Here is the technical schematic of the circuit.
The Arduino pins provide a maximum of 5 Volts. In order to prevent the transistor from being damaged I included a
10K Ohm resistor in the circuit which flows from Pin 9 of the Arduino through the transistor to ground.
The power supply provides 12 Volts. In order to prevent the Arduino from being damaged I included a 10K Ohm resistor
between that power supply, the rotary potentiometer and pin A0.
